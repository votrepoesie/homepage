SPRING 2022

This collection was characterized by the use of soft colors, silhouettes that embody fluidity, as well as employing hand-dyed fabrics. Every single piece was dyed with care and required multiple coloring sessions.

SPRING 2021

I was interested in working with found materials, especially second-hand fabrics from thrift stores and some of my old clothes. Furthermore, quilting was something I enjoyed but never got to experiment with in the form of fashion, which resulted in this colorful quilted jacket.

WINTER 2020

This is the collection that taught me how to sew and construct designs from 2D to 3D with the help of patterns. I had no previous experience besides using the sewing machine to make alterations, and I was drawn to the process of selecting fabrics and combining different textures, which to me was a painterly process. I also took a lot of inspiration from the personalities of my models and what I envision them wearing.
