PAST LIFE
...................

O guardian angel, I want to know the answer to life.

There was a time when past life regression was trending online. My friend sent me a link, and I opened the video (a guided hour-long mediation) with both curiosity and skepticism. To my surprise, the experience turned out to be quite eye-opening. I created these illustrations in an attempt to remember what I had seen and heard.

Of course, there was no guardian angel, only the unconsicous voice in my head that knew much more than me.
