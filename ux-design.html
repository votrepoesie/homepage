Case Study: Smart Museum Mobile App

download pdf

In 2022, I worked on an exhibition at the Smart Museum of Art for a few months, conducting object-based research, writing labels, and recording an audio guide for my favorite work on view. However, these audio guides and resources that the museum has put so much work into were not made obvious to the museum-goer, so I want to reimagine the user experience of visiting and engaging with the museum by creating a mobile app that simplifies information on the website and adds features that improve accessibility and increase engagement.
